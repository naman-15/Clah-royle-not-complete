About Clash Royale

Clash Royale is a freemium real-time strategy video game developed and published by Supercell. The game combines elements from collectible card games, tower defense, and multiplayer online battle arena. Clash Royale first launched with 42 cards, there were 14 cards for each of the three rarities that existed at that time: Common, Rare, and Epic. As of December 2020, there are 102 cards in the game, coming in four rarities: Common, Rare, Epic, and Legendary.
